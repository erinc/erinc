I'd like to specifically talk about Magpie as it's all about automation. Just like Zapier!

A latest trend I've noticed among small businesses is that they've been ignoring email marketing and focusing all their marketing efforts on social media.

Since email is not dead and it's still extremely effective, I thought neglecting email is a mistake and wanted to create a product to tackle this issue. Companies are already generating great content on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and on their blog. So, why not remove all the friction for creating a newsletter by collecting their content and sending them to their customers in a beautiful email?

I quickly created the first mockup [PDF] for MVP with Balsamiq, my favorite mockup tool that I've been using for the last 7 years. After talking to 4-5 people about the product (sanity check) and doing some competitor research, I created the lean canvas (which I do for every product I work own). I used Python and Django (just like you guys) to develop the app by myself in the weekends for a few months. Having a full-stack engineering background enabled me shipping the product without external help. However, I wanted more polish with the frontend and asked a friend to join me in the project. It has been extremely fun working with numerous web service integrations to automate a boring and inefficient process for businesses. Here's how Zapier's fully automated newsletter would look like.
